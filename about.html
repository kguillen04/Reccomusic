about us

Hi all! Thanks for visiting our about us page. We’re a group of first-year students. Here are a couple of fun facts about us: Kevin and Emi met over summer at a Yale program for FGLI students called FSY and Emi and Angelica are roommates in Pauli Murray College! We were super excited to work on this project together because we always try to recommend music to each other, but our tastes can be pretty different. By creating this website, we thought we could find a more accurate way of recommending music to each other–and to anyone else who wants to access our website. Keep reading to find out more about each of our individual music tastes!
